BRICK BREAKER

Play Brick Breaker
Brick Breaker is a Breakout clone in which the player must smash a wall of bricks by deflecting a bouncing ball with a paddle. The paddle may move horizontally. The player loses a life if the ball hits the bottom of the screen. When all the bricks have been destroyed, the player advances to a new level.

Creator: Ali Asaria
Original Release: 1999

Instructions

  1. Use Left Arrow Key and Right Arrow Key to move the paddle in the respective direction and press Escape Key to pause the game.
  2. The ball will bounce off the edges and the ceiling.
  3. You lose a life when the ball hits the bottom of the screen.
  4. You have a total of 3 lives.
  5. Destroy all the bricks to advance to a new level.
  6. When you advance to a new level, the number of lives remaining shall be the same.
  7. Have Fun!
